About Tiny Homes.

Tiny Homes is an Auxiliary Dwelling Unit (ADU) company founded by a local architect, builder, and real estate developer. We specialize in ADUs custom designed to either match your existing real estate or enhance it. Everything we do is customized to add value and enhance the living experience on your property.

The rules have changed. If you own a single-family home on a single-family lot, you are now allowed to build a free-standing tiny home in your backyard.

Many of our competitors are serving this market by making prefabricated, inexpensive, or cheap structures to meet the mass market. Tiny Homes is a company providing upscale small houses for families who have distinguished real estate and want a structure that enhances their property. Our homes are custom designed to match your architectural style or enhance it.

You don't live in a cheap home. We're not providing you a cheap ADU. We have plenty of custom options to fit most budgets.

Contact us, and you will meet the owners.
