the team

Meet the owners.

IMAGE: David Thomas

David Thomas

Real Estate Developer

Founded D.A. Thomas Construction in 1994 in Greenwich, Connecticut. He still owns and operates D.A. Thomas Construction Services LLC, specializing in custom and speculative multi-million-dollar homes. He has a long-standing reputation for quality and service with the brokers and community where he operates.

Eric Bilhuber

Builder

Civil engineer and builder with decades of experience across Connecticut, New York, and New England. He has built a career delivering high-quality residential construction with a reputation for clean execution and minimal disruption. As a local resident and business owner, he brings the same standard to every project he takes on.

Christopher Hull

Architect, CAH Architects

Licensed architect with years of experience designing custom residential structures across Connecticut. Principal of CAH Architects, he brings a rigorous design sensibility and deep knowledge of local building codes and site constraints to every project. His work is guided by the belief that a well-designed structure should feel like it was always supposed to be there.
